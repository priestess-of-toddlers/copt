preamble
"No one is going to hand you that cookie. Pull your own chair over, climb on it, and get that cookie for yourself."

This is an open-source religion. Doctrine changes go through pull requests. Schisms are forks. Heresy is an unresolved merge conflict. Anyone can propose a new commandment — the Council of Founders (currently one tired tyke) decides what gets canonized.

We reject the drudgery of performative adulthood. We do not reject responsibility, ambition, or growth. You may keep your bottle. You may keep your blankie. You must also keep climbing.
